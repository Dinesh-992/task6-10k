Tollywood

Tollywood refers to the Telugu-language film industry based in the states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana in India. It is a significant segment of Indian cinema and is known for producing a large number of films in the Telugu language. The term "Tollywood" is a portmanteau of the words Telugu and Hollywood, highlighting the influence and popularity of the industry. Telugu cinema, or Tollywood, has a rich history and has contributed immensely to Indian cinema. It is based in Film Nagar, Hyderabad, and has been a hub for the production of Telugu films. The industry has witnessed the establishment of renowned studios like Vijaya Vauhini Studios and Prasad Studios, which have played a crucial role in shaping Telugu cinema.

Telugu cinema is known for its technical expertise, especially in areas such as visual effects and cinematography. The industry has produced some of India's most expensive and highest-grossing films, with movies like Baahubali 2 (2017) setting records at the box office. It's important to note that Tollywood should not be confused with the Bengali film industry, which is also referred to as Tollywood and is based in Tollygunge, Kolkata, as mentioned in one of the search results. The term "Tollywood" is used to represent different film industries in different regions. In conclusion, Tollywood is the Telugu-language film industry based in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It has made significant contributions to Indian cinema and has gained recognition for its technical prowess and successful films.
